The user will have 3 types of tools:
a. Axe - for cutting trees
b. Pickaxe - for mining rocks
c. Shovel - for digging dirt
Clicking on a tool followed by clicking on a tile in the world will remove the tile. (If it is of the correct type according to #1) and add it to the inventory.
User can click on the tile in the inventory and place it back in the world (just the last one).
